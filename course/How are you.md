
## To ask people "How are you" in formal and in informal way

**Wie geht es Ihnen ?** - How are you ?  **( Formal way )**

Answer : 
	**Gut , und Ihnen ?**  - Good , and you ? **( formal )**


## Informal way to ask and answer

**Wie geht es dir ?** 
**Wie geht's dir ?**
**Wie geht's** 

All of the above asks - **How are you ?**
